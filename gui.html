To open turtlebot...

  1. Open a new terminal. Export the direction and port of your turtlebot to ROS, as well as your own IP:
        $ export ROS_MASTER_URI=http://<turtlebot_ip>:<port>
    $ export ROS_IP=<your_ip>
  2. In the same terminal, launch web_video_server ROS package:
        $ rosrun web_video_server web_video_server
  3. Access to the following url on your web browser:
    http://0.0.0.0:8080/
  4. Select the image topic to show (it must be the same as the url specified in the html file.)
    • /camera/rgb/image_rect_color
    • /camera/depth_registered/image
  5. Optionally, modify image parameters (quality must be low to achieve a fluent streaming). For example:
    http://0.0.0.0:8080/stream?topic=/camera/rgb/image_rect_color&quality=10
